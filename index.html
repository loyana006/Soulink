Addressing the Gap

Breaking the Silence: Why Soulink Exists

In Nepal, social stigma, high cost, and limited resources prevent many from seeking professional help for mental health. Soulink is designed to overcome these barriers by offering three core pillars of support in a single, accessible platform.
