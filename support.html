A Gentle Safety Net: Emergency & Support Resources

CRISIS DISCLAIMER & IMMEDIATE HELP

Soulink is not a replacement for professional therapy or emergency services.

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or crisis, please contact these Nepal-based emergency services:

  • Nepal Police: 100
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Fire: 101
  • Women Helpline: 1145
  • Child Helpline (CWIN): 1098
  • Mental Health Helpline (TPO Nepal): +977 1660-010-2005
  • Suicide Prevention (Patan Hospital): +977 1-552-1616
  • Suicide Prevention (TUTH): +977 1-441-4740

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How safe is my data?

A: We prioritize your privacy. Private Journal entries are encrypted and kept strictly confidential. Anonymous Confessionals are moderated to ensure a safe community.

Q: Can AI Yana replace therapy?

A: No. AI Yana is an emotional support companion and a tool for reflection. It cannot diagnose or replace a qualified mental health professional.

Q: Is the confession section monitored?

A: Yes, it is lightly monitored to ensure all content adheres to safety guidelines and prevent the posting of harmful or illegal content.
